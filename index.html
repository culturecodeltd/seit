■About Fiber Capital

Since 2018, we have invested in technologies that reduce reliance on intermediaries. Trust in global institutions is eroding. The need for alternative systems is no longer theoretical. AI makes the shift toward trust-minimized systems more urgent and more possible than ever.

We partner with founders who focus on artificial intelligence, cryptography, fintech, healthcare, energy marketplaces, government systems, identity and reputation, the creator economy, and more.

Are you building in these areas? Get in touch with us at info@fibercapital.com.

  • We have collectively started eight companies (six acquired), run billion-dollar business units, invested in 100+ technology startups that now have a cumulative market cap of $50B+, and shipped products used by billions.
  • Our investors are all engineers who ship code regularly.
  • Our experts and advisors have significant expertise in engineering, product design, marketing, HR, liquidity provisioning, governance, and regulatory policy.

This technical foundation helps us evaluate where technology is headed, develop long-term conviction, and provide hands-on support that most firms cannot.

The founders who drive frontier technological advancements must pair deep domain expertise with system-disrupting insights. These founders need a deeply technical partner who can help them navigate structural shifts in technology and markets.

We partner closely at preseed and seed as founders develop product-market fit. We help founders navigate early-stage challenges around design, testing, marketing, and technical implementation.

As teams scale, our experts and advisors help founders evolve products, build teams, develop metrics, manage liquidity, and adjust to ever-changing regulatory complexity. We also connect great founders and communities to one another to advance the entire ecosystem.
